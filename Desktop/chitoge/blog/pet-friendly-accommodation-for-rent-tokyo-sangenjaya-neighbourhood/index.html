Hmlet Sangenjaya is Paradise for Pets & Owners Alike

Living with Pets • Jan 19, 2021

A human of Hmlet and her furry friend find their forever home in Sangenjaya.

We spoke to Hmlet Sangenjaya member and web designer, N, to hear about why she chose Sangenjaya as home for her and her four-legged family member.

All pets welcome

N was looking for a newly built home that was pet-friendly and close to her workplace. "This uncompromisable checklist meant my choices were very limited - until I heard about Hmlet Sangenjaya from my friends. It didn't take long for me to decide to move in!"

On her time off, she goes to Komazawa Olympic Park - a leisurely 15-minute stroll from Hmlet Sangenjaya - which has a dog park. She brings her 14 year old dog, Punch. They also love walking along the greenway just behind Hmlet Sangenjaya to relax. "No cars, extremely calming. I recommend it to all pet lovers."  


Convenience is key

Hmlet Sangenjaya's location was what helped seal the deal for N. It is well-connected by public transport stations, making it a very useful location. Without changing trains, she is able to reach Omotesando and Aoyama easily.

Additionally, there is a 24-hour supermarket, Maruetsu, right in front of Hmlet Sangenjaya. By Sangenjaya Station, there is a Don Quijote Picasso, which offers a variety of household goods. N chirps "Whenever I need anything, I just stroll over and find what I need."

Weekends are for relaxing

Living at Hmlet Sangenjaya, Punch and N are blessed with many pet-friendly cafes nearby. "On our walks, I love stopping at a cafe with Punch." N muses. "We go for a walk to Setagaya Park and make a pitstop at FUNGO right by this park for its excellent burgers." Always crowded with pet lovers, "Punch loves meeting other dogs there!"

Why Hmlet works for Punch and N

Besides being pet-friendly and having an amazing location, N enjoys living with Hmlet because of the additional convenience provided with the built-in services.

N enjoys the ease in which she can order clean sheets "With the Hmlet app, I can order dry cleaning of my bed linens with no fuss." Hmlet's partnership with LUUP - which offers electric bicycles for rent - is also a member perk she relies on frequently. "I can drop off the bike easily as there are dedicated LUUP parking docks at Hmlet Sangenjaya."

She also cites the stylish interior design of Hmlet Sangenjaya as another key reason for her choosing Hmlet. "At Hmlet, I feel like I'm staying in a premium hotel." She adds "I'm all about the brightly lit living room, the chic washroom, large balcony and double doors which gives me confidence in the security."

More than the great location and vibrant neighbourhood, Hmlet Sangenjaya is where N and Punch can enjoy all the creature comforts of home together.
