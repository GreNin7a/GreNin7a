Westside Pet-Friendly Restaurants & Cafes: Neighbourhood Guide

Hong Kong • Jan 08, 2021

Don't want to leave your pets home alone? Head to these dining spots and you won't have to!

It is no surprise that as the temperatures drop in Hong Kong, it gets harder to part with your furry friends who can keep you oh-so warm throughout the day. If you live on the westside, here are some pet-friendly restaurants and cafes so you can take your pets wherever you go!

Pet-Friendly Restaurants

Comptoir

This casual bistro style restaurant under the umbrella of French Creations F&B Group serves traditional Mediterranean tapas with a lively open kitchen vibe. Upstairs, the restaurant transforms into an elegant French wine tasting room, with over 130 bottles to test your palate. Don't forget to book the street-side tables if you are coming with a four-legged friend.

Website | 42 Forbes St, Kennedy Town

Catch

This cozy restaurant is a perfect spot for brunch, especially if you are a fan of fish! Apart from their focus on seafood, Catch's menu is also inspired by seasonal produce, ensuring freshest ingredients in every dish. For the best deal, visit Catch on Monday to benefit from 2-for-1 on all mains!

Website | 95 Catchick St, Kennedy Town

Flying Pig Social Club & Deli

This casual family style restaurant nestled away on Yu Lok Lane, just off Centre Street. A perfect spot for meat lovers, the Deli's menu includes ribs, burgers, spring chicken, and steak. Moreover, the spacious footpath outside the restaurant provides a safe place for your pet to play and mingle with new friends!

Facebook | 52–56 Centre St, Sai Ying Pun

Blue Supreme

Blue Supreme is a gastropub specialising in modern American cuisine and bottle conditioned beers (beers that contain live yeast and cultures that allow flavours to further develop with age). The restaurant's evolving menu prioritises ingredients from local suppliers and small producers in an effort to support local businesses.

Website | 21 Tung St, Sheung Wan

Classified

Opened in 2006, Classified is a casual restaurant with a contemporary design serving homemade artisanal food and gourmet coffees. Classified is a must visit for cheese lovers with its own walk-in cheese room where one can enjoy a wide selection of specialty cheese in a temperature-controlled environment.

Website | 108 Hollywood Rd, Sheung Wan

Pet-Friendly Cafes

Winstons Coffee

Whether you are in the mood for an espresso or an Espresso Martini, Winstons Coffee has got you covered. Apart from being known as the friendly neighbourhood coffeeshop with a killer playlist,  both Winstons' outlets on Hong Kong Island are also pet-friendly.

Website | 11 Davis St, Kennedy Town & 213 Queens Rd West, Sai Ying Pun

First Boy Coffee

First Boy Coffee was established by Ravishing Coffee Solutions, a coffee machine and equipment supplier, so you can bet that their coffee is made from machines of the highest quality. Unlike many cafes where pets are only allowed at the outside areas, here, First Boy Coffee welcomes pets to roam around the coffee shop as they wish!

Website | 33 Praya St, Kennedy Town

ARF Coffee Roasters

ARF Coffee, once known as Artisan Cafe, is located on Po Tuck Street, a small street accessible from Hill Road. This cozy retro style coffee shop was brought to the spotlight after is was used as a venue for a local romantic comedy film, Love in The Buff.

Facebook | 23 Po Tuck St, Sai Wan

No Milkshake No Life

Just as the name suggests, this cafe specialises in milkshakes, from your standard banana chocolate to your not so typical boozy milkshakes like almond caramel Baileys, red wine, and Guinness Oreos. Burgers and grilled cheese occupy the majority of the menu making this the perfect place to check out on a cheat day!

Facebook | 31-37 Hill Rd, Sai Ying Pun

Poise Coffee

Opened in mid0-2020, this new pet-friendly coffee shop in Sai Ying Pun prides themselves in their avocado toast. With a minimalist design, offering a bright and calming ambience and a strong all-day WiFi connection, Poise Coffee is a great spot for people-watching, reading, and working.

Facebook | 1-2, St Stephen's Ln, Sai Ying Pun
